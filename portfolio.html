FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know before working with us.
How long does it take to build a website or web app?
It depends on the complexity. A standard marketing website takes 2–3 weeks. A full-stack web application typically takes 6–12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline estimate during the free discovery call before any work begins.
What is your pricing model?
We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials engagements depending on your project's scope. Fixed-price is ideal when requirements are well-defined; hourly works better for evolving products. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.
Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?
Yes — all projects include 60 days of free post-launch support. After that, we offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing maintenance, feature development, and infrastructure management.
Will there be any additional charges after the website is launched?
Not from us. Third-party costs like hosting, domain registration, and API subscriptions are your responsibility, and we'll guide you on the most cost-effective options. Our development fees are fully agreed upfront.
Do you sign NDAs and provide source code ownership?
Absolutely. We sign NDAs before any project discussion begins. Upon final payment, you receive full source code ownership and all intellectual property rights. Nothing is locked in proprietary systems.
Can you work with our existing team or codebase?
Yes, we frequently augment existing engineering teams or take over legacy codebases. We begin with a thorough technical audit to understand the current state, then create a plan for seamless integration.
